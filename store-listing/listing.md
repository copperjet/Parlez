## Full description (≤4000 chars)
Most apps teach you ABOUT French. Parlez gets you actually speaking it.

Open the app and have a real conversation with Camille, your AI French tutor.
She speaks first, listens to your reply, and answers naturally — the way a
patient French friend would. No lessons, no flashcards, no dashboards. Just
talking, which is the only thing that builds fluency.

WHY PARLEZ IS DIFFERENT
• Conversation first. One screen, one tutor, real back-and-forth from minute one.
• Gentle corrections. Camille weaves the right word into her reply instead of
  marking you wrong. You learn the way children do — by hearing it done right.
• She remembers. Parlez quietly tracks what you struggle with and brings it back
  for practice, so every session is tuned to you.
• Meets you where you are. Total beginner? She leads in English and adds French
  a phrase at a time. Confident? She stays in French and pushes you to elaborate.
• Speak or type. Use your voice, or type when you're somewhere you can't talk.

BUILT FOR A DAILY HABIT
Practice ten minutes a day and watch your streak grow. Show up consistently and
you'll qualify for our money-back guarantee — practice 20 days in a row in your
first month and, if Parlez isn't for you, get a full refund.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your voice is transcribed and immediately discarded — audio is never stored.
What you say stays yours.

Start speaking French today. Bonjour, on commence ?
